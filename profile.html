About Me:

What is there to say, really? My name is Bruce Tonkin, I am 20 years old. I like writing, drawing and watching animated TV shows. I'm studying game design at Wits university, where I am currently a third year.

Above all else, I strive to be a better person every day. I excercise and study and learn new skills to that end, and by always improving, I can always find something new to apply myself to.

To be honest, I don't really enjoy talking about myself. I think that a better way of getting to know me is through my music taste instead. So...

Here's some good music:

Family Ties - Kendrick Lamar and Baby Keem

Arguably one of the best songs of the 2020s so far, I love this song for its bombastic energy and effortless swagger. One of the greatest mainstream debuts ever.

Territorial Pissings - Nirvana

My personal favorite Nirvana song, I feel like their words and lyrics speak to me more the older I get. Also, this song is just so much fun.

Earfquake - Tyler, The Creator.

Tyler, The Creator's journey from hatespeech to making pop-rap with queer imagery has been one of the weirdest journies I've ever watched unfold in music. That journey is probably at least part of why this album is so good.

Mr Brightside - The Killers

I was born in 2002, of course I love Mr Brightside.

Identity Theft - The Chats

You like garage punk, I like garage punk, we all like garage punk. Identity theft is a prime example of the genre, and the weirdly geeky undertones just put it over the top.

Diet Coke - Pusha T

Pusha T's ability to still sound hungry after multiple successful albums is unmatched, and the production on this song just takes it over the top to make it an instant classic.

Heart Shaped Box - Nirvana

Can you tell I like Nirvana? I have played this song so many times on the drums, the infectious energy makes it something I'll always come back to when I need some comfort food.

Meat Grinder - Madvillain

MF DOOM was an unbelievably talented artist before his tragic passing, and this excellent collaboration is one of my favorite products of that talent.

1539 N. Calvert - JPEGMAFIA

A man who needs no introduction. Peggy has slowly been building a reputation for years, and while this may not have been his breakout hit, it still has all his signatures.

Anarchy in the UK - Sex Pistols.

My favorite song to karaoke, Sex Pistols' poser punk just hits me in a way that I kinda wanna copy. I dunno, I just kind of want to do something like this one day. I think it'd be awesome.
